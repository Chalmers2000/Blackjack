📖 How to Play

The Objective

Beat the dealer by getting a hand value closer to 21 than theirs — without going over.

How to Bet

  • Click chips to set your wager (clicks stack)
  • Press Deal to play the round

Card Values

  • 2–10 = face value
  • J, Q, K = 10 points
  • Ace = 1 or 11 (whichever helps your hand)

Your Turn

  • Hit — take another card
  • Stand — keep your total and end your turn
  • Double Down — double your bet, take exactly one more card, then stand (first two cards only)
  • Split — if your first two cards share the same rank, split them into two separate hands, each with its own bet (one split per round)

Dealer Rules

The dealer reveals their hidden card after your turn ends, then must hit on 16 or below and stand on all 17s (no choice involved).

A "natural" blackjack is an Ace + 10-value card on your first two cards (before any split). If the dealer also has blackjack, it's a push.

Win Chance

The corner badge estimates your chance of winning the current hand outright (pushes excluded) by simulating thousands of outcomes from the actual cards left in the shoe. It updates after every card dealt to your active hand.
